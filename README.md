# 💰 Gold Quest: TEKS 7.13D Edition

A high-energy, fast-paced quiz game designed for a 5-minute classroom "exit ticket." Students answer scenarios about **Natural Selection** and **Selective Breeding** to earn gold and reach the high score before the timer runs out.

---

## 🎯 Educational Focus
**TEKS 7.13D:** Differentiate between natural selection and selective breeding (artificial selection).

Students must quickly identify if a scenario is:
* **Natural Selection:** Driven by environmental survival (Nature's choice).
* **Selective Breeding:** Driven by human intervention (Human choice).

---

## 🚀 Quick Start
1.  **Save the files:** Ensure `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` are in the same folder.
2.  **Open:** Right-click `index.html` and "Open with" your preferred web browser.
3.  **Play:** Click the **"Start Lesson Check"** button.

---

## 🎮 Game Mechanics
* **The Timer:** A strict **3-minute countdown** (180 seconds).
* **The Reward:** Correct answers award a random amount of **Gold (10–60 coins)**.
* **The Penalty:** Incorrect answers result in a **20 Gold deduction**.
* **Winning:** Students aim for the highest possible gold count before the "TIME OUT" screen appears.

---

## 🛠️ Customization (For Teachers)

To add more questions or change the difficulty, open `script.js` and edit the `questions` array at the top.

**Add a new question like this:**
`{ q: "New scenario text here...", a: "Natural" },`
*(Use `"Natural"` for nature-based or `"Artificial"` for human-based scenarios.)*
